# Constitutional AI Orchestration Platform - Enterprise Edition

Positioning: the world's first safe, auditable, local AI agent infrastructure for enterprises.

Every enterprise wants AI agents. Every enterprise fears catastrophic automation. This edition combines constitutional safety, sandboxed execution, and GraphRAG memory so teams can deploy agents locally with a full audit trail.
